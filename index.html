Welcome to the NerDS Lab

The Neural Data Science (NerDS) Lab, led by Dr. Eva Dyer at Georgia Tech, builds machine learning methods that help us understand the brain, behavior, and other complex biological systems. We combine neuroscience, AI, and data-driven modeling to uncover representations of neural computation and to build tools for scientific discovery.

Our research is deeply interdisciplinary, bringing together researchers from neuroscience, computer science, and bioengineering to tackle challenges such as cell-type classification, brain-wide decoding, and behavior modeling. We are particularly focused on developing scalable models that generalize across datasets, species, and experimental conditions.
