Welcome to the NerDS Lab

We develop machine learning methods to decode the brain and uncover the computational principles behind behavior and intelligence.

What We Do

  • 🧠 Decode neural activity across brain regions and species using scalable models
  • 🔁 Discover structure in behavior and physiology using self-supervised learning
  • 📊 Build robust models with domain adaptation and representation alignment
  • 🔬 Develop open-source tools for neuroscience and time series modeling (Code)
